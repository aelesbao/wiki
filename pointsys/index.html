    Point system

    In this quest, we introduce the point system:


    1 point is 100 AKT token

    1 AKT = 0.01 USD


    1. Points can be converted to AKT tokens after a certain threshold. E.g., if $1 = 1 point, one will need at least 150 points to convert them to AKT.

    2. Points can be converted to other bonuses, like merch or a visit to meetup, or a certificate, or save them for future community activities (if they don't meet the threshold of 150 points)
